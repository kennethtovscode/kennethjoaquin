About us

Information Technology

The Information Technology program aims to prepare and equip students to effectively utilize hardware and software
technologies in planning, installing, customizing, operating, managing and administering, and maintaining information technology
infrastructure that provides computing solutions to address the needs of an organization.


Information Technology (IT) is a vast field that encompasses the use of computers, software, networks, and
other technologies to manage and process data, facilitate communication, and support business operations.
It plays a crucial role in virtually every aspect of modern life, from business and industry to education, healthcare,
entertainment, and beyond.
